VOLUNTEER US

Our Intiative is supported by many volunteers  accross the globe of who are a core part of our team. 

You can make an amazing difference here, helping to support flood affected people across  Kerala and other affected areas, when they need basic help for surviving. We welcome and value each and every volunteer, who contribute there time in rehabilation and relief of the affected ones.

If you have time to give and the commitment to help the suffering one  we are keen to welcome you among our team. Get in touch with us to find out more. Please write to us at mission@revivingkerala.com

Who can be ?

Anyone who have some time and humanity can join with us. No Gender, Caste, Region or Racial discrimination exists here. So basically anyone can   volunteer with us and expect us to pay off the operational cost 

What Can I do?

There are a number of ways you can support this intiative , from collecting infromation, to charity workshops and marketing and mentoring not limited to surveys and other local supports.

What will I get ?

From the satisfaction of helping someone through a tough time, supporting them through out relief and rehabilation  to learning new skills or meeting like-minded peoples.

Looking for a career with US?

  • Our humanitarian, development and campaigning projects will change lives around the Kerala. Thousands of people are suffering day by day, we need genorous young peoples to help us.

Whatever your interests, experience or skills, we have a role for you, and we will train, mentor and support you in whatever role you choose to do. Are you still planning to start a career with us, Please do write to us at mission@revivingkerala.com
