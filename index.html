Flood Impact

Worst Rainfall and flood in the history.

Kerala experienced the heaviest rainfall since 1924, millions of people remain stranded . The subsequent flooding has left lakhs of houses, bridges and roads damaged, and thousands of families have been forced to take shelter in relief camps.

As Kerala battles its worst floods, Karnataka across the border has reported many landlslides and rainfall deaths. Kodagu, the worst affected region in Karnataka, reportedly accounts for 10+ deaths while over 4,000 people in the district have taken shelter in 41 relief camps.

They need our HELP

Kerala and Kodagu is calling for support. Lives are in danger and high water levels have destroyed all sources of income, lands, farms, and crops  leaving families in desperate need of help. The urgent need on the ground is for long-term support, rehabilitation and rebuilding of homes, water supplies, and other facilities. Without which, the people from economically weaker sections will never be able to get back on their feet. It is our duty to stand with them  to recover this Havoc.
